What is Quizbox

QuizBox is a learning platform that provides you with the means and resources to get you prepared before taking an examination that can really turn your life around. We'v all been students and we know how involving learning can be. In our experience as students we also know that reading your notes and books alone can't really assure you success in your exams but with the practice of past questions you can find out your stregnths and weakness and even know how prepared you are before an examination. Not forgetting to mention the blissfull feeling you get when you come across a repeated past question in the exams.

If practicing past questions helps students pass their exams then why does few students practice?
Boredom, no feedback and after going through the pain to answer the questions, you have to go through twice that pain to mark your self and check the right answers. We'v taken the responsibility to make your learning process more interesting and fun.

How it works

QuizBox is a bank of questions from examination bodies and schools. Quizbox serves you with questions depending on your choice,either from an examination body or a school or a mix of both. You can choose the number of questions and the time for answering the questions.

Things you can do.

Practice

You can practice as many questions as you want from both examination bodies and schools at your own pace.

Challenge

Learning alone is not fun.You can challenge your colleagues on exams questions.

Interact

You can discuss and share your ideas with your colleagues.

Performance Metrics

Instant feedback and records of your performance. You can tell wether you are well prepared for an examination.
